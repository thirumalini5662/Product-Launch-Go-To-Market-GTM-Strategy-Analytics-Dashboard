Product Launch Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project focuses on designing and analyzing a complete Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy for a product launch using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into marketing performance, customer segments, pricing strategies, regional conversions, and campaign effectiveness.

Objectives

- Evaluate product launch performance.
- Analyze marketing channel effectiveness.
- Measure customer acquisition and conversion metrics.
- Compare pricing strategy performance.
- Track ROI and customer satisfaction.

Tools Used

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX
- Data Visualization

Key Metrics

- Total Revenue
- Total Budget
- Total Reach
- Total Conversions
- ROI (%)
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)

Dashboard Visuals

1. Executive KPI Overview
2. Revenue Contribution by Marketing Channel
3. Revenue by Customer Segment
4. Conversions by Region
5. Budget vs Revenue Performance Analysis
6. Revenue by Pricing Model

Business Insights

- Identified top-performing marketing channels based on revenue contribution.
- Evaluated customer segment profitability and conversion performance.
- Compared pricing models to determine revenue impact.
- Analyzed campaign budget efficiency using ROI metrics.
- Assessed regional market performance and customer engagement.

Skills Demonstrated

- Marketing Analytics
- Business Intelligence
- Data Visualization
- KPI Tracking
- Dashboard Development
- DAX Calculations
- Strategic Decision-Making

Outcome

Developed a professional GTM analytics dashboard that supports data-driven product launch decisions and demonstrates practical Business Analyst and Marketing Analyst skills.# Product-Launch-Go-To-Market-GTM-Strategy-Analytics-Dashboard
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze product launch performance, marketing effectiveness, customer segments, pricing strategies, and campaign ROI using a 250-row Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy dataset.
